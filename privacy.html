Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Data Controller

StarConnect Solutions, represented by Stephane Carle (CEO), is the data controller for personal data collected through this website and our services.

Contact: carle@orange.fr | +971 55 224 0899
Location: Dubai, United Arab Emirates

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal data:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, submitted via our contact form or during business correspondence.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, collected automatically for site performance analysis.
  • Application data: resume, cover letter, professional background, submitted by job applicants.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • Responding to enquiries and quote requests.
  • Processing job applications.
  • Providing and improving our services.
  • Fulfilling legal and contractual obligations.
  • Website analytics and performance monitoring.

4. Legal Basis

Data processing is based on: your consent (contact form submissions), contractual necessity (service agreements), and legitimate interest (website analytics, security).

5. Data Retention

Contact enquiries are retained for 24 months. Job applications are retained for 12 months. Technical logs are retained for 6 months. Service-related data is retained for the duration of the contract plus the legally required period.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with:

  • Service providers necessary for our operations (hosting, email).
  • Legal authorities when required by law.

All third parties are bound by confidentiality obligations.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include encrypted communications, access controls, and regular security reviews.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to data processing.
  • Request data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at carle@orange.fr.

9. Cookies

This website uses localStorage to save your language preference. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. No personal data is stored in cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes are effective upon publication on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related enquiry, contact us at carle@orange.fr or +971 55 224 0899.
