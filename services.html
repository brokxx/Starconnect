Interim Solution

While the OmanSat-1 satellite is under development, SCT provides interim satellite communication services to ensure seamless connectivity for government, enterprise, and residential clients across Oman.

Our interim solutions leverage existing satellite infrastructure to deliver reliable broadband, VSAT services, and secure communications throughout the Sultanate, including remote and maritime areas.

Key Services:

  • VSAT broadband connectivity
  • Government secure communications
  • Enterprise satellite solutions
  • Maritime and remote area coverage
  • IoT and M2M connectivity

Omansat-1 Project

The OmanSat-1 project represents Oman's first national communications satellite, a landmark initiative under the National Satellite Program. Airbus Defence and Space has been selected to design, manufacture, and launch the satellite using its advanced OneSat platform.

OmanSat-1 will provide comprehensive coverage across the Sultanate of Oman and surrounding regions, delivering advanced satellite communication services for government, commercial, and residential use.

Project Highlights:

  • Built on Airbus OneSat flexible platform
  • Full coverage of Oman and territorial waters
  • High-throughput broadband capabilities
  • Government and defense communications
  • Satellite imagery and remote sensing
  • IoT and smart infrastructure support
